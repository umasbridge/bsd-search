# Partnership Agreements

1. 5-card majors
2. 2/1 response is game forcing (not by passed hand, not over interference)
3. 1NT response to 1M is forcing for one round (6-12 HCP)
4. Opening light in 3rd seat is acceptable
5. Rule of 20 for borderline openings
